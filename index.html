Referral terms
  • The reward is 20,000 Naira for every friend who enrolls through your link and pays their full course fee.
  • The reward does not apply when any discount, scholarship, or promo is used on the enrollment.
  • For one-time payers, the reward is released after the referred student completes onboarding.
  • For installment payers, the reward is released only after the full balance has been paid.
  • You cannot refer yourself. Self-referrals and duplicate or fake signups are removed and forfeit the reward.
  • A referred student must be new to VAA Global. Existing students do not qualify.
  • Rewards are reviewed and approved before payout. VAA Global may withhold rewards linked to fraud or abuse.
